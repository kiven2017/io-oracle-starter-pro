# 13. Presale

## 13.1 Role of the Presale
The presale provides early-stage capital support for product development, trusted data infrastructure deployment, node-network bootstrapping, market education, and ecosystem partnerships.

## 13.2 Allocation
The Presale allocation is 10% of total supply.

## 13.3 Pricing Framework
- Seed Round: $0.001
- Private Round: $0.002
- Public Sale / IDO: $0.005

Earlier rounds enjoy lower entry prices, but participants also bear longer lock-up periods, lower liquidity, and higher execution risk.

## 13.4 Unlock Structure
15% unlocks at TGE and the remaining 85% vests linearly over 12 months.

## 13.5 Strategic Meaning
The presale is part of early network construction and platform activation, not merely a fundraising event.
