# 14. Governance Framework

## 14.1 Positioning
AIOracle adopts a multi-layer governance model combining foundation-led coordination, protocol governance, and community participation in order to balance security, technical evolution, commercialization, and long-term sustainability.

## 14.2 Governance Participants
- Trusted Network Foundation
- HK BWS Holding Group Ltd., Co
- Node participants
- $AIO holders and community

## 14.3 Governance Principles
Security first, progressive decentralization, reputation- and contribution-aware participation, and long-term value alignment.

## 14.4 Governance Scope
Protocol parameters, ecosystem resource allocation, technical upgrades, and governance-structure evolution.

## 14.5 Proposal and Voting
Governance may distinguish between general proposals, major proposals, and high-risk technical proposals.

## 14.6 Emergency Governance
Limited emergency controls may be used under extreme conditions such as contract exploits, bridge incidents, or large-scale false-data submission.

## 14.7 Treasury Governance
Treasury resources should prioritize network growth, infrastructure security, and onboarding of high-value scenarios.

## 14.8 Evolution Path
The governance system is expected to evolve from foundation-led coordination toward more on-chain governance, delegated voting, and reputation-weighted participation.
