# Executive Summary

AIOracle is a trusted infrastructure network for real-world data, AI inference services, and programmable finance. Its mission is not simply to move off-chain data onto blockchains, but to connect devices, sensors, edge gateways, validator nodes, AI models, and smart contracts in order to generate “on-chain usable physical facts” that can be consumed directly by smart contracts, RWA protocols, and enterprise systems. AIOracle adopts an EVM-first strategy and plans gradual expansion toward Solana and BNB Chain. Its priority real-world sectors are agriculture, logistics, and new energy, with further expansion into high-frequency health-related device scenarios.

From a tokenomics perspective, AIOracle Token ($AIO) has a fixed total supply of 10 billion tokens with 9 decimals. The distribution model is infrastructure-oriented: 40% for node rewards, 15% for ecosystem and treasury, 15% for team allocation, 10% for presale, 10% for liquidity, and 10% for market and advisors. The Node Rewards pool follows a halving-style emission curve every 24 months, while governance may fine-tune the release parameters without changing the long-term total allocation boundary.

This whitepaper presents a full framework covering token utility, tokenomics, presale structure, governance, roadmap, risk disclosure, and strategic conclusion. It explains how AIOracle creates a trusted loop from device onboarding and data signing to edge processing, validation consensus, AI inference, and on-chain publication, and how the project plans to achieve platform operation, cross-chain expansion, ecosystem formation, and capital-market readiness from 2026 to 2028.
