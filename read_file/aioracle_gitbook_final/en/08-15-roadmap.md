# 15. Roadmap

## 15.1 Overall Logic
The roadmap is structured around trusted data infrastructure, sector-scale deployment, the transition from emission-driven incentives toward service-driven node economics, and capital-market readiness with industry-standard influence.

## 15.2 2026 Q2
Complete the Presale, connect 1,000 cross-industry hardware devices, deploy the first nodes, launch the $AIO liquidity pool, and activate the first trusted data pipeline.

## 15.3 2026 Q2–Q4
Connect 15MW of photovoltaic infrastructure and 50,000 health-related devices, standardize onboarding for agriculture, logistics, and new energy, launch AI inference, data cleaning, risk scoring, and state-proof services, and establish the initial protocol revenue → buyback → burn flywheel.

## 15.4 2027
Expand from an EVM-first strategy toward Solana and BNB Chain, connect more than 1,000 ecosystem partners, and establish the broader AIOracle ecosystem.

## 15.5 2028
Begin capital-market preparation and feasibility planning related to Hong Kong and/or Nasdaq pathways, publish industry-level technical frameworks and node standards, and strengthen AIOracle’s standard-setting role in oracle and trusted AI data infrastructure.
