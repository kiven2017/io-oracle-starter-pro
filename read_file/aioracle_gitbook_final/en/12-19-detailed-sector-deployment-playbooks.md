# 19. Detailed Sector Deployment Playbooks

AIOracle does not prioritize sectors evenly. Its deployment strategy follows three filters: high data frequency, high risk value, and high financialization potential. Agriculture, logistics, and new energy rank first because they simultaneously require field devices, multi-source data, risk management, and asset-linked finance.

In health-related device scenarios, AIOracle is not positioned primarily as a consumer wearable layer. Instead, it focuses on continuous monitoring, trusted equipment records, and authorized health-data flows. This can create a highly active data stream, but the commercial path is expected to follow the primary sectors rather than precede them.

## 19.2 Deployment Cadence

In deployment cadence, AIOracle prioritizes scenarios in which devices can be standardized, data can be verified, and demand can recur predictably. This means focusing first on sites with clear device boundaries and frequent state changes before expanding toward larger cross-institutional environments that require deeper compliance and data-governance integration.
