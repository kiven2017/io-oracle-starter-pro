# 23. Glossary and Key Terms

Because AIOracle spans blockchain, device infrastructure, AI inference, RWA, and multi-chain execution, its terminology is more complex than that of a standard token project. A formal whitepaper therefore benefits from an expanded glossary so that readers from different backgrounds can align on the meaning of key terms used throughout the document.

This glossary serves as a reader aid for the financing-edition whitepaper and may be maintained alongside the GitBook and node documentation.
