# 21. Token Release and Vesting Appendix

A formal financing whitepaper should present token allocation and vesting in a review-friendly structure. What matters to investors is not only the percentage assigned to each bucket, but also which portions are liquid at TGE, which remain under cliff restrictions, and which may form medium-term market supply pressure.

AIOracle’s design principle is to keep immediate TGE supply pressure low for nodes and the team, while assigning early market function mainly to liquidity deployment and a limited presale unlock. This supports a healthier balance among price discovery, long-term node incentives, and market confidence.
